About NS Connect

NS Connect is a community-driven organisation based in Nagaland, dedicated to creating opportunities for youth through sports, events, and community engagement.

We aim to connect players, institutions, and communities by building platforms that promote discipline, teamwork, and personal growth, while creating meaningful opportunities for participation and development.

Our Vision

To become a leading platform in Nagaland that empowers youth through sports, community initiatives, and structured opportunities.

Our Mission

• Organise impactful sporting and community events
• Promote grassroots development and youth engagement
• Build strong partnerships with institutions and communities
• Create platforms for talent exposure and growth
