About NS Connect

NS Connect is a community-driven organisation based in Nagaland, dedicated to creating opportunities for youth through sports, events, and community engagement.

We aim to connect players, institutions, and communities by building platforms that promote discipline, teamwork, and personal growth.

Our Vision

To become a leading platform in Nagaland that empowers youth through sports and structured opportunities.

Our Mission

  • Organise impactful sporting events
  • Promote grassroots development
  • Build strong partnerships
  • Create talent exposure platforms
