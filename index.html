About NS Connect

NS Connect is a dynamic, community-driven organisation based in Nagaland, committed to developing sports, empowering youth, and building strong community networks through structured events and initiatives.

We serve as a platform that connects players, institutions, and communities, creating opportunities for talent development, exposure, and long-term growth.

Vision:

To become Nagaland’s leading sports and community platform, empowering youth through innovation, competition, and opportunity.
Mission:
  • Organise high-quality sporting events
  • Promote grassroots youth development
  • Build institutional partnerships
  • Create exposure and growth platforms
